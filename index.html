TIME COLLAPSE GAME









How To Play

Press the "Start Game" button. A box will appear in the middle of the screen with a word inside. A clock will start to count down under the box. Try to tell this word or phrase to your friends without using any actual word in the phrase. Any sound, gesture or movement is allowed. As your friends guess it right, move the device to the next person. The next person will click to the box to get the new word. The procedure repeats. When the time collapse, the person who has the device lose the game.
